FIRST,
THE HEARTH.

We built the restaurant around a living hearth. No gas connections, no electrical hotspots, no thermostats. We cook exclusively with raw split mango, ironbark logs, and red-hot coals.

EVERY GRAIN
OF SALT.

Wild sea salt crystals hand-harvested from the Konkan coast. Crystallized naturally under the sun, providing the clean mineral edge that balances the intense char of the logs.
